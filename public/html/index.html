Easy to Use

Understanding the need to make this service accessible to all the sections of the society, we have tried to make the User Interface as easy to use as possible. All you need to do is select your doctor among those who are online and connect to them on a single click.

group

Freedom to Choose

We provide you the freedom to choose among hundreds of doctors registered on our website. You can view a doctor's profile, his specialisation, his proximity to your place and a lot of other factors vbefore deciding which doctor to connect to.

settings

Convenient

The main aim of this service is to make the entire process of interacting with your doctor convenient. You can book an appointment and make a video call to the doctor of your choice avoiding the hassle of standing in line and waiting outside his clinic.

About Us

We are a small company invested improving the healthcare services in the country. We wish to make the entire process of doctor-patient interaction as convenient as possible. Our main focus is to facilitate video calling facility between the two. This would give patients better accessibilities and far greater convenience.
